Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our repair services

Repair Questions

Most common repairs like screen replacements or battery changes can be completed within 1-2 hours. More complex repairs may take 24-48 hours. We'll always provide you with an estimated completion time when you bring in your device.

Yes, we offer a 90-day warranty on all our repairs and replacement parts. This covers any issues that might arise from the repair work or defective parts.

We repair all major smartphone brands including iPhone, Samsung, Huawei, and other Android devices. Our technicians are trained to handle both current and older models.

Service Questions

While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend making an appointment to ensure we can serve you promptly. You can schedule an appointment through our website or by calling us directly.

Please backup your data before bringing your device in for repair. While we take every precaution, it's always best to have your data safely backed up. Also, remove any screen protectors or cases before bringing in your device.

You can track your repair status using the repair tracker on our website. Simply enter your repair ID, and you'll get real-time updates on your device's status. You can also contact us directly for updates.

Pricing Questions

Screen replacement costs vary depending on the device model and type of screen (original or aftermarket). We offer free quotes, so you can know the exact cost before proceeding with the repair.

We accept various payment methods including cash, mobile money, and bank transfers. For larger repairs, we can discuss payment options to make it more manageable for you.

Data & Privacy

Yes, we take data privacy very seriously. Our technicians are trained to handle devices without accessing personal data. However, we recommend backing up your data before any repair as a precaution.

Yes, we offer data recovery services for devices that won't turn on or have been damaged. Our success rate depends on the extent of the damage, but we'll always try our best to recover your important data.
